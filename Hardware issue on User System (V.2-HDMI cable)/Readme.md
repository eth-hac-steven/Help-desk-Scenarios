# Hardware Issue on User System V2

### Scenario 
  Mr.Johnny (again 🙂) came to the IT department to lay a complaint that his monitor is damaged, because he can`t see anything on it . Investigate  and resolve this.

### Solution
 ### identitfy the issue
 - After a closer look the HDMI cable seems loose. 
 
 ### The Fix
 - Plug it firmly backin
 - Displayed has been restored

With this the Ticket has beenn closed

### Note
   
   There are 3 States that fit this scenario 
 - The display cable could be the issue
 - The power cable could also be the issue
 - The monitor might actually be damaged 🤷🏿‍♂️ (Occurance: rare)
 - ***Bonus***: He did not turn on the monitor

  Display cable can change depending  on the organization but the all do the same thing and they are
   - HDMI
   - VGA 
   - Display port and more recently
    - The USB-C
